Awards

Academy Award for Best Animated Feature

___________

Year: 2003

The Academy Award for Best Animated Feature is given each year for animated films. An animated feature is defined by the Academy as a film with a running time of more than 40 minutes in which characters' performances are created using a frame-by-frame technique, a significant number of the major characters are animated, and animation figures in no less than 75 percent of the running time.



Academy Honorary Award

___________

Year: 2014

The Academy Honorary Award – instituted in 1950 for the 23rd Academy Awards (previously called the Special Award, which was first presented at the 1st Academy Awards in 1929) – is given annually by the Board of Governors of the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS). The award celebrates motion picture achievements that are not covered by existing Academy Awards



Japan Academy Film Prize for Animation of the Year

___________

Year: 2009, 2014

The Animation of the Year (アニメーション作品賞) of the Japan Academy Film Prize is one of the annual Awards given by the Japan Academy Film Prize Association.



Golden Bear

___________

Year: 2002

The Golden Bear is the highest prize awarded for the best film at the Berlin International Film Festival. The bear is the heraldic animal of Berlin, featured on both the coat of arms and flag of Berlin.



Japan Academy Film Prize for Picture of the Year

___________

Year: 1998, 2002

The Japan Academy Film Prize for Picture of the Year is a film award given to the best film at the annual Japan Academy Film Prize.



Golden Lion for Lifetime Achievement

___________

Year: 2005

The Golden Lion is the highest prize given to a film at the Venice Film Festival. The prize was introduced in 1949 by the organizing committee and is now regarded as one of the film industry's most prestigious and distinguished prizes.



Nebula Award for Best Script

___________

Year: 2007

The Nebula Award for Best Script was given each year by the Science Fiction and Fantasy Writers of America (SFWA) for science fiction or fantasy scripts for movies or television episodes. Awards are also given out for published literary works in the novel, novella, novelette, and short story categories.



Kinema Junpo Award for Best Film of the Year

___________

Year: 1989, 2002

The Kinema Junpo Awards for Best Film of the Year is given by Kinema Junpo as part of its annual Kinema Junpo Awards for Japanese films. The award was first given in 1927.



Los Angeles Film Critics Association Award for Best Animated Film

___________

Year: 2002

The Los Angeles Film Critics Association Award for Best Animated Feature is one of the annual awards given by the Los Angeles Film Critics Association.



Silver Scream Award

___________

Year: 2002

The Silver Scream Award is voted for by the audience of the festival in the Imagine Film Festival which is an annual film festival in Amsterdam.



Mainichi Film Concours Award for Best Animated Film

___________

Year: 2001

The Animation Film Award (アニメーション映画賞) is an award given to the best animated feature film at the Mainichi Film Awards. The award was established to reward large scale cinematic animation, enabling the Ōfuji Noburō Award to focus on shorter pieces.



Karl Edward Wagner Award

___________

Year: 2009

Karl Edward Wagner Award is one of the "British Fantasy Awards" which are awarded annually by the British Fantasy Society (BFS). And the Karl Edward Wagner Award is for "important contribution to the genre or the Society" is given at the discretion of the BFS committee.



Kinema Junpo Award, Readers' Choice Award for Best Film

___________

Year: 1985

Kinema Junpo Award, Readers' Choice Award for Best Film is given by Kinema Junpo as part of its annual Kinema Junpo Awards for Japanese films.



Mainichi Eiga Concours Award for Best Film

___________

Year: 1997, 2001

Mainichi Eiga Concours Award for Best Film is an award from The Mainichi Film Awards (毎日映画コンクール, Mainichi Eiga Konkūru). They are a series of annual film awards, sponsored by Mainichi Shinbun (毎日新聞), one of the largest newspaper companies in Japan, since 1946. It is the first film festival in Japan.



Outstanding Directing in an Animated Feature Production

___________

Year: 2002

The Annie Award for Outstanding Achievement for Directing in a Feature Production (or Annie Award for Outstanding Achievement for Directing in an Animated Feature Production) is an Annie Award, awarded annually to the best animated feature film and introduced in 1996. It rewards directing for animated feature films. The recipients are directors as well as co-directors.



Venice Film Festival Mimmo Rotella Foundation

___________

Year: 2008

Venice Film Festival Mimmo Rotella Foundation is an award from The Venice Film Festival or Venice International Film Festival is an annual film festival held in Venice, Italy. It is the world's oldest film festival and one of the "Big Three" film festivals, alongside the Cannes Film Festival and the Berlin International Film Festival.

Our History Our Mission Our Impact
Jen, Kevin, and Amaya established JWK in 2021. It all began when everyone was trapped at home due to the pandemic, and we were all looking for ways to pass the time. One day, the three founders came up with the concept of creating an informative website to alleviate our boredom. Our mission is to use the profits from our websites to help those in need; the more visitors who support us, the more people we can benefit throughout the world. We've given over 200,000 dollars to organizations all across the world, and we're continuously working to make a positive difference in the world.
